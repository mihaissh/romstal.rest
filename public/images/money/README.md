# Romanian Money Images

Place images of Romanian Leu banknotes and coins in this directory as PNG files.

## Required Images

### Banknotes (Bancnote):
- `500-lei.png` - 500 lei banknote
- `200-lei.png` - 200 lei banknote
- `100-lei.png` - 100 lei banknote
- `50-lei.png` - 50 lei banknote
- `20-lei.png` - 20 lei banknote
- `10-lei.png` - 10 lei banknote
- `5-lei.png` - 5 lei banknote
- `1-leu.png` - 1 leu banknote

### Coins (Monede):
- `50-bani.png` - 50 bani coin
- `10-bani.png` - 10 bani coin
- `5-bani.png` - 5 bani coin
- `1-ban.png` - 1 ban coin

## Image Sources

You can find free images of Romanian Leu at:
- [BNR (National Bank of Romania)](https://www.bnr.ro/en/banknotes-and-coins) - Official banknote images
- [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Romanian_leu_banknotes) - Free use images
- [Wikimedia Commons Coins](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Romanian_leu_coins) - Free use coin images

## Recommended Image Specifications

- Format: PNG (with transparency for coins)
- Size: 200x100px for banknotes, 100x100px for coins
- Quality: High resolution for better display
